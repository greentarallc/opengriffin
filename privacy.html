Privacy Policy

Last updated April 14, 2026

Effective date: April 14, 2026

OpenGriffin ("we", "us", or "our") operates the OpenGriffin AI agent platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using OpenGriffin, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Your Telegram user ID (collected when you start the bot)
  • Messages and conversations you send to the bot
  • Memories you explicitly ask the agent to store (names, preferences, context)
  • Tasks you create through the agent
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never see raw card details)

Information collected automatically

  • Word usage counts per model and session
  • Model and agent preferences
  • Billing plan and subscription status
  • Timestamps of interactions

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain your AI agent and conversation history
  • Track word usage and enforce plan limits
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions via Stripe
  • Send proactive scheduled messages (morning briefings, task reminders) if enabled
  • Improve service reliability and debug issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage

Your data is stored on a dedicated server provisioned for your instance. All conversation history, agent memories, and tasks are stored in an encrypted SQLite database on that server. We do not store your data in shared databases or expose it to other users.

OpenRouter processes your messages to generate AI responses. Their privacy policy applies to data sent to their API. Similarly, fal.ai processes image and video generation requests. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: data retained indefinitely while your subscription is active
  • Cancelled accounts: data is preserved for 7 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted
  • You can request immediate deletion at any time by contacting us
  • Use /forget in the bot to delete specific memories at any time

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Stripe: for payment processing (your billing details, subscription status)
  • OpenRouter: your messages are sent to generate AI responses
  • fal.ai: your image/video prompts are sent for generation
  • Legal requirements: if required by law or valid legal process

We never read, sell, or train on your conversation data for our own purposes.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: request permanent deletion of all your data
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability: request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: disable scheduled messages at any time via bot settings

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@opengriffin.com.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Each customer runs on a dedicated server instance with isolated storage. API keys and secrets are stored as environment variables, never in source code or databases.

However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

Our marketing website (opengriffin.com) does not use tracking cookies. We may use minimal session cookies for the Stripe billing portal. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.

Children's Privacy

OpenGriffin is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:

  • Email: hello@opengriffin.com
